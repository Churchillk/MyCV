Resources & Support

If you're struggling, remember that help is available. Here are some resources:

  • School Counselor: Room 204, available 8am-4pm
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255
  • International Support: Check our portal for country-specific resources

Wellness Tips

Small practices for better mental health:

  • Take regular breaks from studying
  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Connect with friends and family
  • Get enough sleep and exercise
  • Limit social media consumption
